## 🔍 Key Insights Uncovered

### 1. The Month-to-Month Risk Factor (Contract Analysis)
* **Insight:** A staggering **42.7%** of customers on Month-to-month contracts churn from the platform. Conversely, customers locked into Two-year contracts show an incredibly low churn rate of **2.83%**.
* **Recommendation:** Introduce a targeted campaign offering an introductory discount to incentivize Month-to-month users to migrate to an annual subscription plan before their 3rd billing cycle.

### 2. The Fiber Optic Infrastructure Alert (Internet Service Analysis)
* **Insight:** Even though Fiber Optic is a premium product, it exhibits a massive churn block compared to traditional DSL lines. 
* **Recommendation:** Flag this technical anomaly directly to the engineering and product QA teams to investigate potential regional service drops, hardware bugs, or pricing friction.

### 3. The Critical First-Year Retention Window (Customer Lifetime Analysis)
* **Insight:** Nearly half of total customer dropouts occur within the first **0–12 months** of signup. If a user crosses the 12-month milestone, their likelihood of staying jumps exponentially.
* **Recommendation:** Focus customer onboarding and proactive customer success check-ins heavily on accounts during their first 6 months to secure long-term loyalty.
